How do I make a return?
Effective 3/12/2020, our return policy has been extended to 90 days. Once our stores are reopened, you are welcome to return your purchase from sunnyglasser.com free of charge to any Sunny Glasser Retail or Company Store. A return shipping fee of USD $9.95 will still be charged for orders returned by mail that use a Sunny Glasser return shipping label.
Do I need an account to place an order?
No. While we highly encourage you to create an account for ease when purchasing online, an account is not needed to make a purchase.
